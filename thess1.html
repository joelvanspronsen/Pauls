Thessalonians 1

Introduction

Written by Paul about 52-52 A.D. One of the earliest written letters Key personalities in this book are Paul, Timothy, and Silas Letter written to strengthen and encourage the church in Thessalonica Paul emphasizes the 2nd coming of Christ Paul also focuses on principles of Faith, Hope, and Love

Sumary

Chapters 1-3: First principles seens as Paul accentuates and commends them for their faithfulness to the Lord. Chapters 4-6: Paul highlights Love and Hope. Encourages the church to walk in love; to (excel still more). Expounds on the return of Jesus and “the day of the Lord.” Paul teaches church about resurrection of on the last day and that Christ will return in the clouds, which encouraged church in Thessalonica. Paul adds to not forget and to pray constantly and “examine everything carefully.”

Important Teachings

Being Christian examples ( 1 Thessalonians 1: 4-10) Living to please God ( 1 Thessalonians 4: 3-12) Jesus will come again ( 1 Thessalonians 4: 13-18)
